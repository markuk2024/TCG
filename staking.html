What is $eVAULT?

$eVAULT is the ecosystem reward token earned by staking $VAULT. It's designed to be used within the TCG Vault Protocol ecosystem, creating a sustainable reward loop.

  • 🎴
    Buy Break Packs Use $eVAULT to purchase pack slots in live breaks (5% discount)
  • 🏦
    Purchase Fractions Buy fractional ownership of vaulted cards with $eVAULT
  • 🎯
    Ecosystem Only $eVAULT can only be spent within the protocol, ensuring value stays in the ecosystem
