How It Works

Four simple steps to start collecting and investing in trading cards

1

Join a Break

Buy pack slots and watch them opened live. Choose a live box break and purchase your pack slot. All breaks are streamed live so you can watch your cards get pulled in real time.

2

Pull Big Hits

If you hit a rare card, you can:

  • Keep it and have it shipped
  • Sell instantly to the vault
  • Grade it for potential profit
3

Vault Collectibles

Rare cards and sealed products are stored in the vault treasury. These assets support the platform ecosystem and provide tangible backing for the token.

4

Own Fractions

Users can buy fractional ownership of valuable cards and trade them in the marketplace. It's like the stock market for trading cards.
